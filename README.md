# 🌱 Sustainable Agribusiness Optimisation (NAB Capstone)

> A consulting-style sustainability project proposing an investment-ready solution designed to secure green financing from NAB.

---

## 🔍 Overview

This project was developed as part of a capstone in collaboration with National Australia Bank (NAB), where the objective was to design a **sustainable, data-informed business solution** for the agribusiness sector.

Focusing on a mid-sized piggery operation, the project addresses critical environmental and operational challenges by transforming waste management into a **renewable energy and revenue-generating opportunity**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Agricultural operations face increasing pressure due to:

* High methane emissions from manure lagoons (up to ~75% of farm emissions)
* Rising and volatile energy costs
* Underutilised waste resources with untapped economic value

These challenges impact both **environmental sustainability** and **farm profitability**, creating the need for scalable, financially viable solutions.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

The project proposes the implementation of a **biogas-based anaerobic digestion system**.

This solution:

* Converts organic waste into renewable energy (biogas)
* Captures methane emissions and reduces environmental impact
* Produces digestate that can be sold as fertiliser
* Supports a circular, waste-to-energy system

---

---

## 📊 Quantified Impact

### 🌍 Environmental Impact

* **70–80% reduction in methane emissions**
* ~**75% reduction in overall farm emissions**
* **100% renewable energy generation** from on-farm waste

### 💰 Economic Impact

* **$25K–$55K/year** revenue from digestate sales
* **$30K/year** savings on electricity costs
* Estimated **~9-year payback period**

These outcomes demonstrate strong alignment with ESG objectives while ensuring long-term financial viability.
